Guidelines for Writing Lesson Notes

  1. Include clear learning objectives for each lesson
  2. Break down the lesson into introduction, main activity, and conclusion
  3. Specify teaching aids and resources needed
  4. Include assessment methods to evaluate student understanding
  5. Note any homework or follow-up activities
  6. Submit lesson notes at least 2 days before the class
